Meet Our Leader

Mr. Ram Prasad Rijal

Chief Executive Officer & Founder

Mr. Ram Prasad Rijal is the visionary founder and CEO of Rijal & Rijal Production. With over two decades of unparalleled experience in the film and media industry, Ram Prasad has a proven track record of bringing captivating stories to life. His deep understanding of cinematic art, combined with a keen business acumen, has propelled the company to the forefront of Nepali film production.

He is passionately committed to fostering local talent, pushing creative boundaries, and showcasing the rich cultural tapestry of Nepal to a global audience. Under his leadership, Rijal & Rijal Production has become synonymous with quality, innovation, and compelling storytelling.

Our Mission

Our mission is to produce compelling and visually stunning cinematic content that tells authentic Nepali stories and promotes the rich cultural heritage of Nepal to a global audience.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading force in the Nepali film industry, fostering creativity, innovation, and technical excellence while creating memorable cinematic experiences that inspire and entertain viewers worldwide.

R&R Production

R&R Production is committed to creating impactful films that showcase the beauty and culture of Nepal.
With a focus on storytelling, we aim to bring fresh and unique perspectives to the Nepali film industry.
Our mission is to inspire, entertain, and connect audiences through the art of cinema, one film at a time.
