Who We Are: The R&R Production Story

Rijal & Rijal Production, often known as R&R Production, stands as a beacon of cinematic excellence in Nepal. Established with a profound commitment to authentic storytelling, our journey began two decades ago with a vision to transform the narrative landscape of Nepali cinema. From modest beginnings, we've grown into a leading production house, consistently pushing creative and technological boundaries.

Specializing in feature films, impactful documentaries, and dynamic advertisements, we meticulously blend artistic vision with cutting-edge production techniques. Our dedication lies in crafting compelling stories that not only entertain but also resonate deeply with audiences, reflecting the rich cultural tapestry of Nepal while achieving global appeal.

Meet Our Visionary Leader

Driving R&R Production forward with passion and expertise.

Mr. Ram Prasad Rijal

Chief Executive Officer & Founder

Mr. Ram Prasad Rijal is the dynamic force behind Rijal & Rijal Production. With over two decades dedicated to the film and media industry, his unparalleled experience and profound understanding of cinematic artistry have established him as a true pioneer. He has a remarkable ability to transform visionary concepts into compelling realities, propelling R&R Production to the forefront of Nepali cinema.

Ram Prasad is deeply committed to nurturing local talent and showcasing Nepal's rich cultural heritage on a global stage. Under his inspiring leadership, Rijal & Rijal Production has become synonymous with innovation, quality, and a relentless pursuit of storytelling excellence that captivates audiences worldwide.

Our Mission

To produce compelling and visually stunning cinematic content that tells authentic Nepali stories, promotes Nepal's rich cultural heritage, and inspires audiences globally through exceptional filmmaking.

Our Vision

To be the leading innovator in the Nepali film industry, fostering creativity, technical excellence, and sustainable practices, while creating memorable cinematic experiences that resonate deeply with viewers worldwide and champion diverse voices.

Rijal & Rijal Production is a leading film production company dedicated to creating cinematic masterpieces that resonate with audiences worldwide.
