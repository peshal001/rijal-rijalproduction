About R&R Production

R&R Production is a leading film production company dedicated to creating compelling and innovative content that resonates with audiences worldwide. With a rich history of successful projects and a commitment to excellence, we continue to push the boundaries of storytelling in the film industry.

  • Our Mission
  • Our Vision
  • Our Values

Our mission is to create exceptional cinematic experiences that entertain, inspire, and connect with audiences on a deep emotional level. We strive to tell stories that matter and make a positive impact in the world through the power of film.

We envision becoming a global leader in film production, known for our innovative storytelling, technical excellence, and commitment to nurturing new talent in the industry.

Our core values include creativity, integrity, collaboration, and excellence. We believe in pushing boundaries while maintaining the highest standards of quality and professionalism.

Our Leadership

Meet the visionary behind R&R Production

Rajesh Rijal

Founder & CEO

With over 15 years of experience in the film industry, Rajesh Rijal has established himself as a visionary leader and creative force in Nepali cinema. His passion for storytelling and commitment to excellence has been the driving force behind R&R Production's success.

Under his leadership, the company has produced numerous award-winning films and has become a prominent name in the industry. Rajesh's innovative approach to filmmaking and his ability to identify and nurture talent have made him a respected figure in the entertainment world.

Key Achievements:
  • Produced over 20 successful films
  • Recipient of multiple national and international awards
  • Pioneer in introducing new technologies in Nepali cinema
  • Mentor to numerous emerging filmmakers
