Kaira

A romantic Nepali movie exploring love, luck, and heartbreak across Kathmandu and the Philippines.

About Kaira

Kaira is a romantic Nepali movie that delves into the lives of Jay (Aaryan Sigdel) and Kaira (Samragyee Shah), two individuals with luck in all aspects except love. Their journey begins with a serendipitous meeting at a bar in Kathmandu and takes them to the scenic Philippines, where their love story unfolds amidst challenges and emotional twists.

The first half engages with the duo's budding friendship and comedic moments, while the second half takes a more intense, emotional turn. Despite some predictable elements, the chemistry between the leads and the stunning cinematography make it worth watching.

Details

  • Runtime: 145 minutes
  • Release Date: August 3, 2018 (Nepal)
  • Director: Laxman Rijal
  • Writer: Kedar Bhusal
  • Genre: Romance
  • Language: Nepali
  • Production Companies: Aaryan Sigdel Entertainment, R & R Production

Top Cast

  • Aaryan Sigdel: Jay
  • Samragyee Rajya Laxmi Shah: Kaira
  • Shishir Rana
  • Hitesh Sharma
  • Tsuresh Neupane
  • Eddie Edwards
  • Ajay: Narendra Singh Dhami

Highlights

The movie features excellent performances, particularly by Samragyee Shah in emotional scenes, and Aaryan Sigdel's natural, calm delivery. The cinematography by Shivaram Shrestha showcases the beauty of the Philippines. However, the script lacks originality, with certain sequences feeling stretched or inspired by other films. The music, with contributions from Sugam Pokharel, Yubraj Chaulagain, Ketan Chhetri, and Shreya Sotang, stands out as a high point.

Box Office

Gross Worldwide: $5,518

December Falls

A suspenseful story of a nurse navigating struggles for justice and love.

About December Falls

December Falls movie tells the suspenseful story of a nurse who faces emotional and professional challenges as she struggles for justice and love. With gripping twists and intense moments, the film keeps the audience on the edge of their seats while exploring the depth of human emotions.

Details

  • Runtime: 2 hours 10 minutes
  • Release Date: December 30, 2022 (Nepal)
  • Director: Pravin Jha
  • Writer: Kedar Bhusal
  • Genre: Thriller
  • Language: Nepali
  • Production Company: R & R Production

Highlights

The movie boasts an engaging storyline filled with suspense and emotional depth. The direction by Pravin Jha ensures a captivating narrative, while the performances bring life to the characters. With a budget of NPR 30,000,000, the production quality stands out, delivering a visually appealing and intense experience.

Budget: NPR 30,000,000 (estimated)
